Whats the Issue?


A lack of clean and affordable water leads to health risks in the youth and elderly. Even healthy adults and teens are affected by dirty drinking water. Hundreds of thousands of deaths are caused every year due to dirty drinking water, and millions become sick and unable to provide for their family and community.
