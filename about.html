There are 663 million people on the planet who don’t have access to clean water.


That’s nearly 1 in 10 people worldwide.

% of Deaths from Unsafe Water

SDG 6.1 emphasizes the need to achieve universal and equitable access to safe and affordable drinking water for all. Universal allows for the geographical equality in that every part of the world should have access to sanitized and safe drinking water. Equitability on the other hand focuses on making the drinking water available and affordable for everyone no matter what conditions may be present.
